Hello!
Objective: reach the last green row without losing all of your three lives. 
Don't run into the cars in the road or go into the river without being on a log.
Also dont go out of bounds or you'll lose a life. 
if you lose all three of your lives, its game over.

Controls: up = up; 
down = down;
left = left,
right = right

Hard Mode: to toggle on/off hard mode, press B.
when hard mode is activated, you cannot go left or right, so plan accordingly.
hard mode is on when the indicatorB is yellow.
hard mode is off when the indictor is red.
